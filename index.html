Behind the story how Tech Café was invented

The café was founded in Hoboken by four students from Stevens Institute of Technology who wanted to open a modern aesthetic cafe for leisure or studying.

In addition to our full espresso menu, we serve fresh made-to-order breakfast and lunch sandwiches, as well as a selection of sides and salads and other good stuff.
